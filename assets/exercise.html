Importance of Exercise

Whatever your age, there's strong scientific evidence that being physically active can help you lead a healthier and happier life. Physical activity or exercise can improve your health and reduce the risk of developing several diseases like coronary heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, cancer, and cardiovascular disease. Regular activity can improve your quality of life.

Benefits:

  • Better mood
  • Better brain function
  • More money in your wallet
  • Lower risk of heart disease and stroke
  • Lower risk of type 2 diabetes
  • Lower risk of some cancers
  • Longer life
  • Stronger bones and muscles

Running

Benefits of Running:

  • Helps build strong bones (weight-bearing exercise)
  • Strengthens muscles
  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Burns calories
  • Helps maintain a healthy weight

Push-ups

Benefits of Push-ups:

  • Improves upper-body strength
  • Increases core stability
  • May improve cardiovascular health
  • Enhances sports performance
  • Works multiple muscles at once
  • Supports bone health

Swimming

Benefits of Swimming:

  • Relaxes and reduces stress
  • Improves coordination, balance, and posture
  • Improves flexibility
  • Low-impact therapy for injuries
  • Great way to cool down

Bicycling

Benefits of Bicycling:

  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Increases muscle strength and flexibility
  • Improves joint mobility
  • Decreases stress levels
  • Improves posture and coordination
  • Strengthens bones
  • Reduces body fat
  • Helps prevent diseases
